About us

Pre - Construction Services

Success begins early in the development stage to build a firm foundation for your project. To maximize success, Paradigm Construction offers pre-construction services to provide value added construction expertise and an understanding of the process and cost. Working with the owner, architect, and engineers, we formulate a feasible, cost-effective budget and develop an achievable schedule. Our pre-construction services allows us to review the design documents, provide input on design details and materials selection, and offer value engineering services on the selection of alternate materials and systems.

Construction Management

Paradigm’s Construction Management services provide our expertise with construction methods and materials, project scheduling, and cost to the process during the design phase. Working together as partners we help our clients and their designers develop a program that maximizes their investment. Construction Management services such as construct ability analysis and life cycle cost analysis provide information necessary for the client to make informed decisions. We also implement project controls to keep your project on schedule and within budget, using creativity and imagination to overcome obstacles that may arise.

Traditional General Contracting

With our extensive background in public and private general contracting, Paradigm acts as an “At-Risk” General Contractor responsible for field supervision of subcontractors, financial management, purchasing and scheduling of all materials and trades, contract negotiation, and contract management. We view this role as a natural extension of the partnerships we develop with the client and their design professionals during the planning phase of the project.
